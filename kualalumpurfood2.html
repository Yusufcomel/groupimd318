Wira Seafood Ikan Bakar Sedap


Cockles are a popular delicacy in Malaysia, and one of the best places to enjoy them is at Wira Seafood Ikan Bakar Sedap in Batu Caves. This humble eatery has been serving up mouth-watering seafood dishes for years, and their cockles are a must-try for anyone looking to indulge in the local cuisine. At Wira Seafood Ikan Bakar Sedap, the cockles are served with a spicy sambal sauce that perfectly complements their natural flavor. The sambal is made from a blend of chili peppers, garlic, shallots, and other spices, giving it a fiery kick that is sure to satisfy even the most adventurous eaters.

18, Jalan SG 1/10, Taman Sri Gombak, 68100 Batu Caves, Selangor
