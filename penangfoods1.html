Shong Hor Hin


Spanning over 50 years, this family-run business has never encountered a dip in quality in their impeccable choices of herbal teas. Once you step into this prewar building, you’re instantly greeted with the rousing aroma of herbs and welcoming owners. This medicated shop serves bitter tea, bamboo cane tea and chrysanthemum tea infused with honey. Cherry pick one and blissfully sip in a punch of herbs that cools down body heat and jostles away other regular health concerns. Their special desserts which vary daily are praiseworthy too.

194 Lebuh Kimberley, 10200 , Georgetown, Penang


Every day

04:00PM—11:00PM

Tropical Spice Garden


What about enjoying a shot of herbal tea in between a realm of herbs? Head down the mesmerizing coastline of Teluk Bahang till you reach Tropical Spice Garden, among the spots that should never be missed once touching down the island. Be amazed at lush greenery, lovely ponds, fresh breezy air, enthralling herbal leafs and the tea hut. Amidst Mother Earth's undisturbed majesty, grab few glasses of herbal tea that kicks in an earthy and minty taste that gets you going once again. Moreover, its free-flow, you can definitely come back for more.

Lot 595 Mukim 2, Jalan Teluk Bahang, Teluk Bahang , 11100 , Georgetown, Penang


Every day

09:00AM—06:00PM

Winter Warmer


With numerous outlets in Penang, Winter Warmers ticks all criteria for an unusual gastronomy delight. This restaurant becomes a piece of your home as the English-style decoration exudes relaxing vibes at every sight. With smorgasbord choices of teas that are imported all the way from Germany, you’ll be spoilt with choices. Their herbal tea is served in vintage jars and glasses, filled with aromatic herbs and light, it’s a clear winner once it taps onto your tongue. For a hint of lemon, opt for their Lemon Verbena herbal tea and pair it with a slice of Lavender cheesecake, indeed match made in heaven.

Gurney Plaza, 170 G38, Persiaran Gurney, 10250, Georgetown, Penang


Every day

09:00AM—10:00PM
