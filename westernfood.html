Jake’s Charbroil Steaks


It was always a treat then and now whenever I dined at Jake’s. They have earned their place as one of the best steakhouses in KL by virtue of service and quality. There are options for those who prefer a non-beefy affair with the likes of lamb, chicken and fish. With perfect steaks and a little something for everyone, Jake’s will never disappoint.

Ben’s


A contemporary interpretation of where East-meets-West, Ben’s has earned its place as one of KL’s best. This cosy restaurant is the perfect setting for both friends and families to meet for any occasion. Their motto “To Eat is to Love”, shows their commitment to building relationships and bringing quality meals to the table. When all you can think about is to relax and unwind over a classic meal, then Ben’s is the perfect place for you.

Lot 37A and 37B, Level G2, No. 1 Jalan Dutamas 1, Solaris Dutamas, Publika, 50480, Solaris Dutamas, Kuala Lumpur


Every day

Acme South


Stepping into this café is like walking into an upscale New York apartment. From the same team that brought you Acme Bar and Coffee, Acme South is an award-winning eatery with a focus on cuisine from America’s Deep South. Apart from serving a mouth-watering array of Southern-style fare, Acme South has recently added to its menu several dishes closer to home with a unique Asian twist to tickle even the fussiest of tastebuds.
