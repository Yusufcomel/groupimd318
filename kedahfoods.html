Intan Payung Cafe


Intan Payung Cafe is a cozy and welcoming establishment that offers a perfect blend of comfort and elegance. Nestled in a serene location, the cafe is adorned with charming decor, featuring soft lighting, comfortable seating, and tasteful artwork that creates a relaxing atmosphere. The menu is a delightful mix of local and international dishes, with a focus on fresh, high-quality ingredients. From aromatic coffees and teas to delectable pastries and hearty meals, there's something for everyone. The cafe also boasts a selection of refreshing beverages and homemade desserts, making it an ideal spot for both casual gatherings and special occasions. Friendly staff and excellent service add to the overall pleasant experience at Intan Payung Cafe.

17 Jalan Bujang Indah Bedong, Kedah

Nasi Kandar Baginda


Nasi Kandar Baginda is a popular dining spot known for its authentic and flavorful Malaysian cuisine, specializing in nasi kandar, a traditional dish featuring steamed rice served with a variety of rich and aromatic curries and side dishes. The menu offers succulent chicken, tender beef, fresh seafood, and vegetarian delights, with standout dishes like fried chicken, fish curry, and squid sambal. The casual and inviting ambiance, combined with friendly and efficient service, makes it a great place for a relaxed meal with family or friends. Despite its bustling atmosphere, the restaurant maintains cleanliness and provides quick service. Offering excellent value for money, Nasi Kandar Baginda ensures generous portions and reasonable prices, making it a favorite among locals and tourists alike for a true taste of Malaysian cuisine.

39, Jln NCity 1/1, N-City Business Park, 08000 Sungai Petani, Kedah


Open 24 Hours

Restoran Terapung Segantang Garam


Restoran Terapung Segantang Garam is a unique dining destination that offers an unforgettable experience with its floating setup and picturesque views. Specializing in fresh seafood, the restaurant serves a wide array of dishes, from succulent grilled fish and prawns to mouth-watering crabs and calamari, all prepared with a perfect blend of spices and local flavors. The serene ambiance, with gentle breezes and the soothing sound of water, enhances the dining experience, making it ideal for both romantic dinners and family gatherings. The staff is known for their warm hospitality and attentive service, ensuring that every guest feels welcome and well taken care of. Prices are reasonable, especially considering the high quality and freshness of the food. Overall, Restoran Terapung Segantang Garam provides a delightful culinary journey, combining delicious seafood with a unique and relaxing atmosphere.

Kuala Segantang Garam Merbok, 08100 Sungai Petani, Kedah
