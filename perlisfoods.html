Restoran Api Api Ikan Bakar


Restoran Api Api Ikan Bakar offers a delightful dining experience for seafood enthusiasts. Renowned for its perfectly grilled fish, the restaurant boasts a menu rich with fresh, flavorful options that tantalize the taste buds. The Ikan Bakar, marinated with a blend of aromatic spices and cooked to perfection over an open flame, is a standout dish that captures the essence of traditional Malaysian cuisine. Complementing the main courses, the variety of sambals and side dishes provide a burst of flavor that enhances the overall meal. The casual, welcoming ambiance and attentive service make it an ideal spot for both family gatherings and casual outings with friends.

Jalan Dato Ali, Bukit Tok Po, 02000 Kuala Perlis, Perlis

Kedai Ikan Bakar Perlis Qaseh


Kedai Ikan Bakar Perlis Qaseh is a gem for seafood lovers, offering an authentic and satisfying dining experience. The highlight of their menu is the Ikan Bakar, which is expertly grilled and seasoned with a unique blend of spices that capture the traditional flavors of Perlis. The freshness of the fish, combined with the smoky, charred aroma from the grill, makes for an unforgettable dish. Alongside the fish, the restaurant serves a variety of delicious accompaniments, including tangy sambals and flavorful sides, all of which enhance the meal. The cozy and inviting atmosphere, coupled with friendly and efficient service, ensures a pleasant dining experience, making Kedai Ikan Bakar Perlis Qaseh a must-visit for anyone in the region.

Kampung Bintong, 01000 Kangar, Perlis


Wednesday, Thursday, Monday, Friday, Sunday, Saturday

10:30AM—11:30PM

Restoran Nyiru Klasik Ikan Bakar


Restoran Nyiru Klasik Ikan Bakar offers a remarkable culinary experience, specializing in the art of grilled seafood. The restaurant's signature dish, the Ikan Bakar, is a culinary masterpiece, prepared with fresh fish marinated in a distinctive blend of spices that highlight the rich flavors of traditional Malaysian cuisine. The grilling process imparts a smoky, charred essence that complements the fish's natural taste. Accompanying the main dish are an array of delectable sides and spicy sambals that add a burst of flavor to the meal. The restaurant's warm and rustic ambiance, combined with attentive and friendly service, creates a welcoming atmosphere that enhances the dining experience. Whether for a family gathering or a casual meal with friends, Restoran Nyiru Klasik Ikan Bakar is a destination that promises satisfaction and a true taste of Malaysia.

Asputra Ikan Bakar Kuala Perlis


Asputra Ikan Bakar in Kuala Perlis is a haven for seafood lovers, offering an exceptional dining experience with its expertly grilled fish dishes.
