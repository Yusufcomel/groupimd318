Asam Pedas Premier


Planted in One Precinct Bayan Baru, Assam Pedas Premier is the place to be if you’re looking to charm your taste buds with authentic bowls of assam pedas. Sidestepping from usual humble-looking joints, one can expect a warm and cosy atmosphere. Prices do vary according to the selection of fish – stingray, mackerel and kembong line up as contenders, and it would be wise to opt for set meals which include salted egg, spicy sambal belacan and ulam. The combination of flavours downright delivers in each spoonful, thus you’ll be surprised, in a blink of an eye the bowl is polished clean. If you need another reason to visit, their nasi lemak ayam berempah is another showstopper.

Menara KOMTAR, 10450 George Town, Pulau Pinang, Malaysia, 11950, Georgetown, Penang

T.H Leong Seafood


This renowned stall has garnered a loyal following over the years for their smorgasbord of seafood offerings that are pretty delectable. If you’re trying to drudge through the day or looking to perk up a gloomy mood, everything on the menu offers a helping hand, especially their assam pedas fish. Be awed with the portion as a generous amount of vegetables interweave with fresh chunks of fish, striking a partnership that is nothing short of succulence. Triumphing in both taste and texture, there is a lot happening on your taste buds, leaving you wanting more.

Jalan Tan Sri Teh Ewe Lim, Jelutong, 11600 George Town, Pulau Pinang, Malaysia, 11600, Georgetown, Penang

De Pauh Garden Restaurant & Cafe


Hopping over to mainland, to a restaurant that presents an exhibition of mouth-watering delights each and every day. De Pauh Garden Restaurant is easily identified with huge red awnings and white-washed alfresco dining space. Once you've stepped in, pan your sight from left to right, scanning all the mind-boggling array of food that amps up your hunger, and if assam pedas is your jam, you’re in for a treat too. Served steaming in a claypot, a hotchpotch of fish fillets, tomatoes and okras tie together to deliver mouth-watering flavours. It’s good to go with a mound of white rice but other sides like fried chicken, mixed vegetables and salted eggs vie for equal attention.

Jalan Permatang Pauh, 13500 Permatang Pauh, Pulau Pinang, Malaysia, 13500, Butterworth, Penang


Every day

10:30AM—02:00AM
