Yin's Sourdough Bakery & Cafe


What we loved about Yin's Sourdough Bakery & Cafe is the fact that they make their own set of bread from scratch. This includes the long process of natural fermentation of the sourdough bread, the type of flour used and of course, the lack of additives and preservatives. Each bread or bun is carefully made to utmost standards and flavour. The cafe is also rustic and undeniably beautiful.

11, Pesara Claimant, 10100, George Town, Penang


Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday

08:00AM—05:00PM

Jenni Confectionaries


Jenni Confectionaries is one of Penang's older bakery and cafe. Unlike most cafe that focuses on slightly more modern and contemporary cakes, Jenni Confectionaries does simplier and customized cakes. Do note that the cakes are sightly on a sweeter side. Apart from that, it might also just be the perfect place to purchase cakes for kids!

33-A, Jalan Cantonment, 10350, Pulau Pinang, Malaysia, 10350, George Town, Penang


Every day

09:00AM—09:00PM

Love A Loaf Bakery Cafe


Love A Loaf will have loving a cup of coffee too. Upon arrival, you will be greeted by the fresh scent of yeast and butter alongside a subtle aromatic scent of coffee. Grab the coffee and enjoy it with your choice of artisanal bread!

1-1-16, Tingkat Mahsuri, 11950, Bayan Lepas, Penang

Mon Delice Patisserie


This classy French sounding place has much to offer. Aside from delivering fresh bakery pastries, they too have a host delicious entrees and appetizer if you're craving for more. It's located in a magnificent traditional long house with a spellbinding central courtyard - a great form of escapism while feasting on delightful treats!
